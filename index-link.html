Our Mission

Our mission is to create an inclusive community online. This website functions as an all inclusive forum for health. We are starting with the topics of birth control and reproductive rights. This website will expand to other topics such as medication, surgeries, and self care while having reliable resources linked to each topic. Teenagers and adults can come to Link and find the care they need while discussing procedures that work best for them, in order to avoid past mistakes. Link bridges the gap, by providing reliable health resources, using discussion forums and reviews.
